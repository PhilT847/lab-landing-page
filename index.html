We can't afford to lose our salt marshes.
Salt marshes store carbon, prevent flooding, and sustain diverse plant and animal life. At the Marsh Lab, we're researching methods to preserve these critical ecosystems before they disappear.
What We're Asking
How do historical ditching efforts affect the shape of NJ's marshes?
How is fertilizer damaging marsh plant communities?
How can we establish stronger root systems to stabilize marshes?
How long can low marsh species survive under rising sea levels?
